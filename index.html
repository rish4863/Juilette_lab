Introduction

A lot of web designers design forms for an organization so people can register for an event in an easy way such as a conference. In this example sometimes the instructions on how to register for a conference may not be clear. When you go to this website and click down on the registration tab you expect to see a registration form. However, there is one problem which is there is not a clear button for the registration form. This is important for people who wants to register quickly. I plan on fixing that so that people will have better access to the forms such as, how long they are staying for, where they are from, who are they representing, and what hotel they are staying at. Some things that the website can do is to include more visuals so that the users can understand how the form works. One thing that can also help is using more informative text. Good web design also includes informative links that tells the user where they need to go.
